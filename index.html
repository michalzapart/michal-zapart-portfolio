About me
As a UX Design Lead & AI Strategist with 14+ years of experience, I focus on making complex technology feel human. I specialize in leading teams, conducting deep user research, and building Design Systems that actually work for people.
My "superpower" is bridging the gap between design and engineering — I speak both languages fluently.
I bridge the gap between design and production by combining UX expertise with full-stack development (Python, C#). This allows me to ensure AI-driven products are inherently human-centric. From architecting AI workflows to mentoring designers, I love navigating the future of product design.
