NavLog
NavLog is an application used for navigating commercial passenger aircraft during flight. I contributed to this project as the lead UI designer.
White labeling
The application was designed using Adobe Illustrator and fully engineered to support white-labeling ecosystems (showcased here with screenshots from the EasyJet custom profile).
NavLog
The application also natively supports dedicated dark mode and high-contrast theme variations.
The dark theme optimizes flight crew readability during nighttime operations, while the high-contrast variant ensures perfect display visibility during high-altitude cruising under intense, direct sunlight.
