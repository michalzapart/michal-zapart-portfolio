RealValue
RealValue was a very special project for me, as I had the opportunity to prove myself as a workshop facilitator for clients and as a UX Researcher.
Assumptions & Objectives
The goal was to create a user-friendly application for people looking to invest in real estate as well as property managers.
Since a user could have more than one role, the transition between the investor persona and the manager persona had to be seamless.
Investments
Investments are presented in two ways: as tiles containing essential details, and as a list view.
Search options and the displayed investment metrics were carefully selected together with stakeholders during the UXR process.
Property View
The view includes property photos designed to encourage users to invest or lease.
The most important information about the property is displayed below the photos, varying depending on whether it is a purchase, rental, or investment.
